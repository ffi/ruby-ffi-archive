Next weekend, I suspect.

Now that 1.9.2 is out, I will be dropping support for anything older
than 1.9.2  Although it will most likely work fine with any 1.9.x, and
maybe even 1.8.7, I will personally only be making sure 1.9.2+ works.
Older versions will only get support if people send in patches.

I don't want to drop support for existing versions in a point release,
so it will be version 0.7.0.

To anyone who has raised a ticket in the ruby-ffi github tracker, and
_has_ attached a patch and I haven't applied it to master, speak up
now, or forever hold your peace - it means I overlooked your patch.
